Our Journey Through Time

Nearly a century of growth, adaptation, and unwavering service to Ottawa

1927

The Beginning

Reg Bruce begins operations as a coal merchant with the solid belief that active community involvement would be rewarded. The foundation of what would become Bruce Fuels is laid in Ottawa's growing landscape.

Growth & Diversification

Reg Bruce's holdings expand to include hotels, restaurants, and construction assets. His dedication to Ottawa is recognized with both a conservation area and residential community bearing his name.

Second Generation Leadership

Grant McDonald takes the helm, focusing operations specifically on fuel services as the energy landscape evolves. The company adapts to changing market demands while maintaining its community focus.

1990

Reid McDonald Era Begins

Reid McDonald, Reg's grandson, joins the company bringing fresh energy and industry experience from Kemp Fuels. His vision for proper operations and customer service drives the company forward.

1993

Leadership Excellence

Reid McDonald becomes General Manager, bringing decades of experience and a commitment to running operations with integrity, efficiency, and customer focus.

2025

Today & Tomorrow

Bruce Fuels continues to evolve with state-of-the-art technology, comprehensive services, and the same family values that started it all. Ready for the next century of service to Ottawa.

Since 1993

Reid McDonald

General Manager & Third Generation Owner

Reid McDonald represents the third generation of Bruce family leadership in Ottawa's energy sector. As Reg Bruce's grandson, Reid brings both family heritage and extensive industry expertise to his role as General Manager.

Having been involved in the fuel industry for most of his adult life, Reid gained valuable operational experience at Kemp Fuels before returning to Bruce Fuels in 1990. This external experience provided him with fresh perspectives and industry best practices.

Since becoming General Manager in 1993, Reid has successfully managed the company's evolution while maintaining the family values and community commitment that define Bruce Fuels. His leadership has guided the company through changing energy landscapes and technological advances.
