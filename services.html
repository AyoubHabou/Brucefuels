Before You Call Emergency Service

If your oil furnace won't start, try these simple troubleshooting steps first

Quick Troubleshooting Checklist:

  1. Check thermostat setting - ensure it's set above room temperature and on "heat" mode
  2. Verify furnace switch and breaker are in "on" position
  3. Check oil supply and ensure valve is open
  1. Check furnace breaker or fuses in electrical panel
  2. Press Red reset button - "Press once only"
  3. If none work, call us immediately
